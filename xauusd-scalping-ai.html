Executive Summary

What makes V19 different

V19 retains the battle-tested three-timeframe engine from V18 and adds a complete Smart Money Concepts (SMC) filter layer that runs independently on M15. Every potential entry must pass both the classical momentum gate and the SMC gate — entries blocked by exhaustion, sweep context, POC distance or VWAP placement are rejected before the order is ever sent to the broker.

V19 Smart Money Filters

Five new institutional guards

SMC 01

Trend Exhaustion — Z-Score

Tracks the length of every completed bull and bear streak on M15. When the current streak's Z-score exceeds 2.0 standard deviations above the historical mean, the trend is flagged as statistically stretched and new entries are blocked. Prevents chasing parabolic moves about to reverse.

SMC 02

Liquidity Sweep Detection (BSL / SSL)

On every new M15 bar, the EA scans for buy-side (BSL) and sell-side (SSL) liquidity grabs using a configurable lookback window. A BSL sweep blocks long entries; an SSL sweep blocks short entries — keeping you out of traps where retail stops have just been hunted.

SMC 03

CHOCH — Change of Character

A sweep followed by an opposite entry signal = CHOCH. When detected, the EA resets the Anchored VWAP to the current bar, resets the H4 cycle tracking, and emits a Telegram alert. This forces a clean re-evaluation of market structure instead of clinging to the previous directional bias.

SMC 04

Volume Profile POC

Accumulates tick volume across ATR-sized price buckets on M15 (up to 100-bar window) to identify the Point of Control — the most-traded price level. Entries are blocked when price is more than 3× ATR away from the POC, ensuring you trade near institutional value rather than in thin air.

SMC 05

Anchored VWAP

A VWAP accumulator anchored to the most recent CHOCH event. In a BUY trend, buys are blocked when price is below AVWAP — buying below institutional value. In a SELL trend, sells are blocked when price is above AVWAP. The anchor resets automatically on every new CHOCH.

Risk Architecture

Capital preservation layers

  • Session FilterTrades only London (07:00–16:00) and New York (12:00–21:00) — peak institutional liquidity windows only.
  • Daily Profit TargetPauses trading once the daily target is hit (default $600). Locks gains before the market reclaims them, and optionally closes all open positions.
  • Daily Loss LimitSame mechanism on the downside (default −$600) — no catastrophic drawdown days. All positions are closed and the EA pauses until tomorrow.
  • Consecutive Loss BlockAfter 5 consecutive losses, trading halts. Combined with a 1,200s (20-min) cooldown — prevents revenge trading and emotional over-trading.
  • Spread FilterRejects entries when spread exceeds 65 points. Protects against news spikes and thin liquidity environments.
  • Exhaustion Guard (V19)Z-score of streak length automatically blocks entries when the trend is statistically over-extended — no more chasing a move that's already run.
  • Liquidity Sweep Guard (V19)Prevents trading with the sweep direction after a BSL or SSL grab — keeps you out of the most dangerous reversal traps in institutional price action.
  • Max Trades Per SessionHard cap of 22 trades per session day prevents over-trading even when the EA is on a signal run.
