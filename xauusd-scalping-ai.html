Risk Architecture

Capital preservation layers

  • Session FilterTrades only London (07:00–16:00) and New York (12:00–21:00) — peak institutional liquidity.
  • Daily Profit TargetPauses trading once daily target is hit (default $600). Locks gains before the market reclaims them.
  • Daily Loss LimitSame mechanism on the downside (default −$600) — no catastrophic drawdown days.
  • Consecutive Loss BlockAfter 3 consecutive losses, trading halts. Combined with 600s cooldown — prevents revenge trading.
  • Spread FilterRejects entries when spread exceeds 35 points. Protects against news spikes and thin liquidity.
  • Broker Stop-Level ComplianceSL is always above broker minimum + 10pt buffer — orders are never silently rejected.
